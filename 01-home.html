About Me

A practitioner, not just a consultant.

With over a decade of experience at the intersection of corporate strategy and sustainability, I help organisations translate ambitious environmental and social commitments into real, measurable outcomes — for people, for business, and for the planet.

I have worked across 45 countries, partnering with multinational corporations, development finance institutions, and public sector bodies to embed ESG principles into decision-making, governance, and reporting frameworks — including CSRD, TCFD, and GRI standards.

My approach is grounded in rigour, shaped by lived experience on the ground, and driven by a genuine belief that business can be a force for good. I bring both the strategic lens and the operational depth to make it happen.
